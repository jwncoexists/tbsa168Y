April 28th - May 3rd, 2015

In July of 1967, 219 Marine lieutenants assembled at The Basic School at Quantico, Virginia. Basic Class 1-68 will conduct a reunion of our classmates on Tuesday, April 28th through Sunday, May 3rd, 2015. It has been nearly 48 years since we were together at Quantico -- this promises to be a special occasion indeed.

Fredericksburg, VA

Reconnect with your TBS A-168 classmates in historic Fredericksburg, Virginia. Join us for numerous special events including a memorial service at the Vietnam Wall. While in Fredericksburg, make sure to visit the homeplace of George Washington, and walk in the footsteps of Civil War generals.

For our Fallen

Sadly, 49 of our class members are no longer with us. We lost some in combat and others later in life. Each was taken too soon. Family members of those who've fallen are invited to participate in reunion activities - especially the memorial service that we will hold at the Vietnam Wall. Several of our widows have signed up early to join us, and we hope many more will do so.
